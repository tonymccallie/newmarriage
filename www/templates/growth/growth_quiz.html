Self-Care Quiz

Self Care…. Most people today have a different understanding about Self Care. You may define Self Care as getting a lot of exercise, a balanced diet, prayer and meditation, lots of rest, etc…. All of these are a part of Self Care, but not all that you need. What we want to help you understand is that Self Care is about life balance. So you may ask, “ what does a balanced life look like?” We believe that scripture gives us the guide to this question. Jesus was asked what is the greatest commandment and He said, “love the Lord your God with all you heart, mind, soul, and strength. The second commandment is to love your neighbor, as you love yourself. Those are the four areas (mentally, physically, spiritually, and emotionally) that Jesus gave us as an example of how to love God and love ourselves. In Luke 2:52 scripture says, “And Jesus grew in wisdom (mind) stature (body), and in favor with God (soul) and man (heart).

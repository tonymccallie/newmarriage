“Us” Problem Solving Steps

The following are the Five Steps for Problem Solving. Remember to always use these with your spouse in creating a win/win solution for the “US”.

STEP 1
Commit to stay together in the process and on the solution.
STEP 2
Talk about possible solutions.
STEP 3
Pray together.
STEP 4
Pick a solution.
STEP 5
Be willing to choose a different solution.
